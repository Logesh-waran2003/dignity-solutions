Multi-City Operations

Daily Productivity Reports

The DPR system helps track daily collections against estimated targets. Each FOS attends 15 cases per day with systematic follow-up of PTP customers maintained in our system.

Receipt & Audit Control

Stat Card and receipt book control with daily verification. Every receipt issued is recorded in the system with registered case allocation date-wise and collector-wise.

Skip-Tracing Expertise

Specific and trained executives with expertise in tracing delinquent customers who are absconding, using information from neighborhoods, workplaces, contacts, friends and various agencies.

Target-Driven Operations

Executives are given targets for calling, visitations and PTP conversions on a daily basis. Broken promises are visited jointly by supervisors with executives on alternate days.
